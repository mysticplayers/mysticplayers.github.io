About Us

Our Mission

At Mystic Players Revival, our mission is to bring the magic of live theater back to Medford, MA by reviving dreams, one performance at a time. We believe theater has the power to inspire, entertain, and connect people. Through a mix of classic works, bold new productions, and community events, we strive to create a welcoming space where artists and audiences come together to celebrate the transformative spirit of the stage.

Get Involved

Mystic Players Revival thrives because of the people who share their time, talent, and support. Whether you’re performing, volunteering, or helping behind the scenes, there’s a place for you in our theater family. By getting involved, you help us bring bold productions and meaningful opportunities to Medford—keeping the spirit of live theater alive for artists and audiences alike..

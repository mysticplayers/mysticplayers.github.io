President

Margaret Petrella

I was born and raised in Medford, MA, and theater has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. My mother was a singer her entire life, and I was lucky enough to grow up surrounded by music, creativity, and a whole lot of show tunes. I performed with Arlington Children’s Theatre, Medford Public Schools drama club, and just about anyone who would let me sing. Theater and singing weren’t just hobbies — they were the only things I truly knew how to do, and they gave me a place to feel at home. I also grew up with a grandfather who did so much for this community and believed deeply in helping others and just being a good person. That stuck with me. For a long time, I asked myself how I could carry that same spirit forward in my own way — and eventually, the answer came. I started a small group called “Bring Community Theater Back to Medford,” and with a lot of heart, a little chaos, and the support of my amazing board members, Mystic Players Revival was born! We’re more than just a theater company — we’re a family. I’m so proud of what we’re building here in the city that raised me, and I can’t wait to share it with all of you!
